Home made Chicken Lasagna

Delicous and creemy chicken lasagna made at home

Ingredients:

  • Chicken chest
  • Plate cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Dough (home made or bought)

How to prepare:

  1. Cook the chicken chest and shred it
  2. Add the tomato sauce to the shredded chicken
  3. Star laying the dough into a mold.
  4. Alternate between one layer of dough, one of chicken and one of cheese
  5. The top layer has to be made of dough to serve as a cover
  6. Put it into the oven for 45 minutes
  7. Done!

Extremely good as a weekend special!
